## Electrical Plug / Outlet :electric_plug:
 
[Electrical Plug and Voltage Information for Austria](https://www.power-plugs-sockets.com/us/austria/) 

In Austria, power plugs and sockets (outlets) of type F are used. The standard voltage is 230 V at a frequency of 50 Hz.
