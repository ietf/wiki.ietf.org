# A Semantic Definition Format (ASDF)

The ASDF WG has been operating mostly through monthly virtual interim meetings, with some plenary week meetings.
Most virtual interim meetings are being scheduled for 9am Finland (Eastern Europe) in order to overlap easily with Korea and California (midnight).  (This is obviously poor for East-Coast North America)

Visit https://datatracker.ietf.org/wg/asdf/meetings/ for details.
