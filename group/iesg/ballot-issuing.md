# Issuing Ballot
Once a document completes IETF Last Call and the responsible Area Director is satisfied with it, they should issue a ballot in the Datatracker.

Issuing the ballot for a document prompts the Secretariat to add it to the next available telechat agenda.

The ballot is issued via Datatracker from the document information page.
