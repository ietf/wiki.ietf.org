# Important resources and links for Liaison Coordination at a glance

### General
* [IAB webpage: Liaison coordination](https://www.iab.org/role/liaison-coordination/)
* [Liaison coordination issue tracker on Github](https://github.com/intarchboard/liaison-coordination/issues)
* FAQs: https://chairs.ietf.org/liaison-faqs

### Liaison statements
* [RFC4053](https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc4053.html) and [draft-iab-rfc4053bis](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-iab-rfc4053bis/)
* IETF datatracker liaison management tool: https://datatracker.ietf.org/liaison/
* [Wiki on Updated Process for Handling Incoming Liaison Statements](https://wiki.ietf.org/group/iab/process_incoming_liaison_statements)

### Liaison manager management

* [IETF webpage: liaison relationships incl. liaison manager table on the IETF webpage](https://www.ietf.org/about/liaisons/)
