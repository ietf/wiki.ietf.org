# 3GPP liaison relationship

The liaison manager from 3GPP to IETF is always the CT chair.

3GPP maintains liaison resources here:

* Liaison Statements: https://www.3gpp.org/about-us/liaisons-statements
* IANA registration request tracking: https://www.3gpp.org/delegates-corner/ietf-ieee/iana-v2
* Draft & Obsolete RFCs cited in 3GPP specifications: https://www.3gpp.org/delegates-corner/ietf-ieee/rfcs-ietf
