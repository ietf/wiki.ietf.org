### 1. Discuss Draft ISE Consulation Survey
Document: [Draft IAB ISE Survey](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ZsFvatT16cyh05DTN0NlQ0n9wZyDJlbDbaPZJ-peKm0/edit?tab=t.0) 
Sub Group: Jason, Jana, Alvaro, Roman

Key Questions:
1.	Survey purpose: What is the primary goal of the survey:
(a) to support the IAB’s selection of the next ISE, or
(b) to gather broader community input on the ISE role and its evolution?

2.	Survey scope: Should the survey focus on:
(a) qualifications/criteria for ISE selection (e.g., building on RFC 8730), or
(b) broader questions about the ISE role, responsibilities, and publication scope (RFC 4846/RFC 8730)?

3.	Relationship to RFCs
Should the survey be used to inform potential updates to RFC 4846 and RFC 8730, or remain independent of any immediate revision effort?

4.	Survey methodology
How should the survey balance:
(a) quantitative inputs (to assess distribution/strength of views), and
(b) qualitative input (to capture reasoning behind positions)?
