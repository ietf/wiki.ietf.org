# BGP Administrative Drafts
Some IDR draft are administrative.  These drafts include:
- definition of IANA Registries, 
- methods to assign Extended Communities, 
- methods to algorithmically assign protocol numbers. 

These IDR Draft do not require two implementations. The reasons for each of these drafts is included below. 

## Drafts
### Drafts at IESG
#### draft-ietf-idr-rt-derived-community
[draft-ietf-idr-rt-derived-community](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-ietf-idr-rt-derived-community) [-09] 

The IDR chairs granted this draft administrative status based on this statement: 

>    While in the above example, an RT-derived EC is used for the purpose
>    of importing routes to a VRF configured with the corresponding Route
>    Target, this document only specifies a way to derive an Extended
>    Community from a Route Target Extended Community by switching its
>    Extended Community sub-type (or Extended Community type in case of
>    IPv6-Address-Specific Extended Community [RFC5701]) to "RT-derived-
>    EC".
