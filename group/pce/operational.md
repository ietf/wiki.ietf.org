# Operational Considerations for PCE WG Drafts

All PCE WG drafts needs to include an **"Operational Considerations"** section as per the guidance in [draft-ietf-opsawg-rfc5706bis](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-ietf-opsawg-rfc5706bis/).

In the past, the PCE WG has used [RFC 6123](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc6123#section-2.4)’s *"Manageability Considerations"* as a template. Going forward, authors should instead follow the updated guidance in [RFC5706bis](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-ietf-opsawg-rfc5706bis/). Note that RFC 6123 is Historic.
