# Operational Considerations for PCE WG Drafts

All PCE WG drafts needs to include an **"Operational Considerations"** section as per the guidance in draft-ietf-opsawg-rfc5706bis.

In the past, the PCE WG has used RFC 6123’s *"Manageability Considerations"* as a template. Going forward, authors should instead follow the updated guidance in draft-ietf-opsawg-rfc5706bis. Note that RFC 6123 is Historic.
